The branch

Who we are

River Plate Copenhague is the official branch of Club Atlético River Plate in Denmark. We are a non-profit voluntary association founded by Argentine fans and friends of the club living in Copenhagen.

We organise sports activities, social events and represent the institution within the Argentine community in Denmark.
